Safety for All.

At VoyCera, the wellbeing of our customers is above everything else.We are constantly in the pursuit of enhancing our safety measures to ensure that every voyage is pleasant and comfortable experience.



Covers Everyone

For Customers


Every ride is tracked by Voycera with access to granular latitudinal and longitudinal data.

For Captains


From hiring to training to monitoring to ongoing checks, we take all necessary steps to ensure our Captains safety.



Measures to ensure the well-being of both, our Captains and Customers.

24 X 7 Customer Support

Both, our customers and captains can report any kind of issues to VoyCera through the 24*7 feature on the app post and during the ride.

Insurance

Insurance can be claimed for any accident that occurs during the ride covering OPD treatment, hospitalization, and accidental benefit with a maximum sum insured of 5 lakh rupees. It can be claimed as soon as the ride.

Two way Rating System

post the ride both parties can rate and any rating below 3 is flagged from VoyCera's end.We reach out to them in 10 minutes to address their concern.
